allyzon

Filipino startup Allyzon, is an online service that delivers fresh groceries
at your doorstep. It is not only seeing phenomenal sales growth but is also
getting adequate attention from investors. Recently the app received
100 billion won or about $88 million in Series D funding from its existing investors.
Allyzon got its funding from investors at home and abroad including Capital
China, Global Venture Partners based in London and Berlin and Mirae Asset
Venture Investment in Korea. The startup plans to utilise the investment to
expand its supply chain area and increase its labour force to deliver more
and better stuff.
